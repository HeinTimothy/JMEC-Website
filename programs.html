Get a professional quality, dedicated working space, confernenceing rooms, and expert mentorship. This program is for purdue founders in need of an HQ space while they grow their company.

Seed Incubator Program

A Space for Innovation
The incubator program provides Purdue entrepeneurs with a space to develop their business. Within this program, founders will be able to establish and adress for their HQ, hold conferences with potential investors, use the workspace, and receive mentorship from our experts. The space is located at Wang Hall, 516 Northwestern Avenue Suite 4008, West Lafayette, IN 47906.

Benifits

A Home for your Company

As a member of the incubator program you get a dedicated workspace for your company. This includes office space, collaboration spaces, and a conferenceing room. This space is designed to be used as an HQ for your company to help you grow and expand without needing to invest in real estate.

Expert Mentorship

As a member you will have access to a network of mentors ranging from industry experts to former founders to seasoned investors.

Professional Conferenceing Space

Wang hall is outfitted with professional tier conferencing spaces. These spaces may be used by your startup in order to hold meetings with potential investors, clients, or other parties that will help move your business forward.

Community of Founders

Working in this space will place you directly in a community of founders and entrepeneurs. Being in this community will expose you to a diversity of skills, views, and experiences that will help you develop as a founder.

Summer Student Accelerator Program

A Driver of Growth
The Summer Student Accelerator is Purdue's campus startup accelerator which is designed to help student run companies bring their product to market. The primary purpose of the program is to develop an MVP for demonstration to seed-investors. The program lasts 3 months from late May to early August and in located in the John Martinson Entrepeneurship Center.
